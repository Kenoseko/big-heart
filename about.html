Our Story

About Big Heart

Creating joyful spaces where children learn, imagine, play, and grow into their best selves.

🎯
Our Mission

What We Do Every Day

To nurture children's imagination, creativity, and love for reading through meaningful stories, engaging toys, active play, and interactive learning experiences that develop the whole child — emotionally, physically, and intellectually.

🌟
Our Vision

Where We're Headed

To raise a generation of imaginative, confident, and well-rounded children across Africa — children who read widely, think creatively, play joyfully, and lead with empathy and purpose in their communities.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

From a single reading circle to Kenya's leading child development platform.

2019

The Beginning

Big Heart started as a small Saturday reading circle in Nairobi with 12 children and a dream to make reading fun and accessible.

19
20
2020

Going Digital

Adapted during the pandemic to virtual storytelling sessions, reaching over 300 children across Kenya through online programs.

2021

First School Partnerships

Launched formal partnerships with 8 Nairobi schools, embedding reading culture programs into school curricula.

21
22
2022

8 Programs Launched

Expanded to offer 8 distinct programs covering storytelling, sports, creativity, wellness, and innovation, reaching 1,000+ children.

2023–2025

National Growth

Expanded to Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru. Reached 2,500+ children, partnered with 48 schools and 6 NGOs across Kenya.
