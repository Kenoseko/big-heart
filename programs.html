Program 01

StoryLab

Our flagship storytelling and reading program that transforms children into passionate readers and confident storytellers through immersive, interactive sessions.

Ages 4–14 📅 Saturdays 9–11am 🏫 In-Person & Online
What Children Do
Live storytelling by professional storytellers
Book-of-the-week discussions and quizzes
Children create and perform their own stories
Author meet-and-greet sessions
Story illustration and comic strip creation

Program 02

PlayLab

A hands-on creative play program using toys, games, and imaginative activities that develop fine motor skills, problem-solving abilities, and social skills.

Ages 3–8 📅 Sundays 10am–12pm 🏫 In-Person Only
What Children Do
Structured and free-form toy play
Sensory play stations
Cooperative board games
Imaginative role play scenarios
Puzzle-solving challenges

Program 03

ReadCircle

A structured literacy program that builds reading fluency, comprehension, and a genuine love for books through guided reading clubs, challenges, and events.

Ages 6–16 📅 Weekday After-School 🏫 Schools & Centres
What Children Do
Guided reading circles with age-appropriate books
Monthly reading challenges and leaderboards
Book reviews and recommendation writing
Library visits and book fair participation
Reading aloud and comprehension exercises
Enroll in ReadCircle →

Program 04

ActiveHub

An energetic sports and movement program that keeps children active, builds teamwork, develops sportsmanship, and promotes physical health and vitality.

Ages 5–16 📅 Saturdays 8–10am 🏟️ Outdoor Venues
What Children Do
Multi-sport sessions (football, netball, athletics)
Team-building games and relay challenges
Junior sports tournaments
Fitness and movement circuits
Sports leadership development
Enroll in ActiveHub →

Program 05

Builders

A hands-on STEM and arts program where children design, build, and create using recycled materials, LEGO, electronics, and stop-motion animation tools.

Ages 7–14 📅 Sundays 2–4pm 🛠️ Workshop-Based
What Children Do
LEGO and structural engineering challenges
Recycled material art and sculpture
Stop-motion animation projects
Basic electronics and simple circuits
Design thinking and prototyping workshops
Enroll in Builders →

Program 06

InnovationHub

A showcase-oriented program where children develop and present creative projects — from inventions to business ideas — building public speaking and entrepreneurial skills.

Ages 10–16 📅 Monthly Showcases 🎤 Presentation-Based
What Children Do
Develop original innovation projects
Public presentation and pitch skills
Mentorship from entrepreneurs and educators
Collaborate in cross-age project teams
Showcase projects to families and schools

WonderNest

A gentle, discovery-based program for our youngest learners, nurturing curiosity, sensory exploration, early literacy, and boundless imagination in a warm, safe setting.

Ages 2–6 📅 Saturday Mornings 🏡 Parent-Inclusive
What Children Do
Sensory play and discovery stations
Read-aloud and picture book sessions
Music, movement, and nursery rhymes
Nature and outdoor exploration
Parent-child bonding activities

Program 08

MindSpace

A holistic wellbeing program focused on emotional intelligence, mindfulness, self-expression, and mental health awareness for children in a safe, supportive group environment.

Ages 6–16 📅 Sundays 11am–1pm 🧠 Wellness-Focused
What Children Do
Guided mindfulness and breathing exercises
Emotion journaling and expressive art
Group discussions on feelings and challenges
Yoga and gentle movement for children
Confidence-building activities
