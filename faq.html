Questions? We've Got Answers! 🙋🏽‍♀️

Everything parents need to know about Big Heart.

What ages can join?

We have programs for kids as young as 2 all the way up to 16! Check our Programs page to see what fits best.

Do parents need to stay?

For kids under 5, parents join the fun! For older kids, you are welcome to drop them off with our trusted teachers.

What should my child bring?

Just a water bottle and a big smile! We provide all the books, art supplies, and sports gear.
